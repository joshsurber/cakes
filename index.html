My name is Tina Orosco. I have had a passion for baking as far as I can remember. Baking sparked this joy inside of me that I couldn't get enough of. Anyone who loves baking knows what I mean.

Back in the early 90’s I got my first job in bakery at Safeway and developed a passion, not only for baking but for cake decorating. I would watch the cake decorators at work and was amazed at how beautiful the cakes looked. I started teaching myself. I learned really quickly and before long I was head cake decorator, a title I carried at several bakeries.

When not working as a decorator, I still made cakes on the side for family and friends celebrating birthdays, weddings and other special occasions. I have been decorating and baking for more than 31 years and still enjoy learning; there are so many new cakes I would love to make! Decorating to me is a work of art and very fulfilling. I’m thankful that God has given me this passion and talent to spread joy with cakes.

I live in San Antonio. I’m an associate Pastor’s wife, mother of 5 (plus 4 bonus step-children!), grandma to 7 beautiful grandchildren with one more on the way.
